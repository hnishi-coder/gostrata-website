About Gostrata Ltd

A licensed and regulated international brokerage company committed to delivering professional financial services.

Company Overview

Gostrata Ltd is a licensed international brokerage and financial services company incorporated under the International Business Companies Act 2014. We operate under the regulatory oversight of the Mwali International Services Authority (MISA), providing professional financial services to clients worldwide.

Our company holds an International Brokerage and Clearing House License, authorizing us to offer a comprehensive range of brokerage, asset management, and clearing services in global financial markets.

Regulatory Framework

Gostrata Ltd is incorporated under the International Business Companies Act 2014 and holds an International Brokerage and Clearing House License (No. BFX2026018) issued under the Brokerage Act 2013 by the Mwali International Services Authority (MISA) of the Autonomous Island of Mwali (Moheli), Comoros Union. The company is subject to the regulatory requirements and ongoing compliance obligations set forth by MISA.
